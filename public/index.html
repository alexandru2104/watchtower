Questions

Before you ask

How fast are alerts?

Prices are checked every minute. The moment your threshold is crossed, you get a Telegram message within that same minute — no polling required on your end.

Which coins are supported?

The major ones — BTC, ETH, SOL, BNB, XRP, ADA, DOGE, MATIC, DOT, LTC — with more added based on demand.

Do I need to keep a tab open?

No. Watchtower runs on our servers, not in your browser. Close the tab, close your laptop — the alert still fires and still reaches your Telegram.

What's the actual difference between Free and Premium?

Free alerts fire once, then deactivate — you recreate them to keep watching. Premium alerts stay armed and can fire again (with a 30-minute cooldown so you're not pinged every minute), plus you get unlimited alerts and a full history of every past trigger.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, whenever you like. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period, then drop back to the Free plan automatically.
